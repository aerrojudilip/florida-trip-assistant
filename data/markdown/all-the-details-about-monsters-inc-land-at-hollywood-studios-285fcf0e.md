What We Know About Monsters, Inc. Land at Disney's Hollywood Studios
During the Horizons: Disney Experiences Showcase at D23, Disney made the exciting announcement about an up-and-coming "Monsters, Inc."-themed land. Disney Experiences Chairman Josh D'Amaro, joined by Disney Legend (and voice of Mike Wazowski) Billy Crystal revealed the plans.
Experience all four Walt Disney World Theme Parks this summer with the 4-Day, 4-Park Magic Ticket! Valid for arrivals from May 26 to Sept. 26, 2026.
The new area will bring the world of "Monsters, Inc." to life, allowing guests to walk the streets of Monstropolis alongside their favorite monsters. The land will pick up after the original film's events, inviting humans into the monster world for the first time.
After the world-changing discovery that human laughter is far more powerful than screams, monsters began to not only grow curious about humans, but desire a connection with them. Enter the Department of Human Relations — a new team established by the city of Monstropolis tasked with helping monsters better understand humans as welcomed guests.
The first order of business for this unorthodox HR Department: establishing H.U.M.A.N. (short for "Humans Understand Monsters Are Nice") Day. Part celebration, part cultural exchange, this day of festivities is designed to introduce humans to life inside Monstropolis while helping monsters discover that humans aren't so frightening after all.
During its inaugural year, human guests will have the opportunity to explore Monstropolis, shop its stores, meet its scaly and furry citizens, taste unique cuisine — including some grub at the infamous Harryhausen's where Mike and Celia had their ... memorable ... date — catch a show at the Glob Theater and step into the everyday world of monsters in a completely new way.
As for the main attraction, you're going to want to hold on tight! Disney's first-ever suspended coaster will take guests on a thrilling ride through the iconic vault door. Hey, they scare because they care. ;) Josh D'Amaro expressed his excitement for the project, saying he has dreamed of riding through the door vault since the movie's release in 2001. Monsters Inc. Land will also host an all-new theater show.
At SXSW 2025, Disney revealed a first look at the loading station. Just like how the claw snatches up the doors in the movie, the roller coaster will utilize a vertical lift before sending riders off into the Monsters, Inc. factory.
Closures and Refurbishments to Make Way for Monsters, Inc. Land
Currently, there is no set opening date. However, construction is scheduled to begin in summer 2025, which meant that a few experiences were closed to do so. Grand Avenue area began a phased closing in May 2025 to make way for the monsters. The last day to dine at Mama Melrose's Ristorante Italiano was May 10. Then in June, Muppet*Vision 3D and PizzeRizzo closed June 8, with June 7 as the last day to experience them.
But don't worry — the Muppets have moved to Sunset Boulevard to headline Rock 'n' Roller Coaster! This new overlay opened on May 26, 2026, and we absolutely adore what they've done to the iconic G-Force Records.
