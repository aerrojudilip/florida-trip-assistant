Disney Dining Plan Options
When purchasing a Disney World vacation package, you'll have the option to add on a Disney Dining Plan. These popular meal plans can only be purchased as part of a Disney World vacation package. In 2026, you can choose from the Quick Service Dining Plan, the Disney Dining Plan, or choose not to add on a dining plan at all.
Here is what is included with each Disney dining plan option in 2026 — each member of your party ages 3 and over will receive the following per night of stay:
Quick Service Dining Plan
- 2 quick-service meals
- 1 snack/nonalcoholic drink
- 1 Resort-refillable drink mug (for length of stay)
Disney Dining Plan
- 1 quick-service meal
- 1 table-service meal
- 1 snack
- 1 Resort-refillable drink mug (for length of stay)
Note that, in 2027, your dining plan options change a little! Next year, you'll have an additional dining plan option to consider: Deluxe Table Service Dining Plan. This dining plan includes:
- 2 table-service meals
- 1 quick-service meal
- 1 snack/nonalcoholic drink
- 1 Resort-refillable mug (for length of stay)
The Disney Dining Plan becomes the Table-Service Dining Plan in 2027, with the inclusions above. The Quick-Service Dining Plan remains unchanged from 2026 to 2027.
